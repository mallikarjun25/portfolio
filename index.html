About Me

I’m Mallikarjun Aitha, a data professional who genuinely enjoys making sense of messy, real-world data. Right now, I work as a Data & Geospatial Analyst with the City of New Haven, where I turn raw datasets into insights that actually help people make better decisions. Whether it’s building dashboards, designing ETL pipelines, or digging into spatial patterns, I like connecting the dots between data and impact.

Over time, I’ve worked across corporate and public sectors, which has shaped how I approach problems—with both technical depth and practical perspective. I’m especially drawn to projects where data and geospatial tools can improve how cities function and how communities thrive. From analytics to machine learning, I’m always looking for ways to use data not just intelligently, but meaningfully.
